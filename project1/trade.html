The Pentel Energel Clena is well known for its retro coloring and carnival-esque stripes. However, it is also well known for being one of the best writers that has come from the Pentel company. This pen is their peach-colored variation, featuring a 0.3mm rollerball tip, and a nice black ink. This ink dries in a very similar color to it's original writing, and is a perfect long time writer with it's extra comforatble tip. you can be assured that this pen will give you a smooth glid every time.
